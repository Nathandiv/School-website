Date Published 16 January 2025

POSITION: High School Life Science & Natural Science Teacher
EMPLOYER: Shaper High
LOCATION: Pretoria, South Africa

Shaper High is a Co-Educational Independent School from Grade 000-12, situated in the East of Pretoria. The school places a large emphasis on providing a holistic education that supports the development of well-rounded individuals.

We invite applications for the position of Life Science (FET phase) & Natural Science (Junior Secondary phase) Teacher in the High School with effect from April 2025. Applicants should have the necessary qualifications and experience and be a SACE registered Teacher. A suitable candidate should be prepared to engage fully with the co-curricular programme of the school. The successful applicant will take up a teaching load in the High School together with the other required duties.

The applicant should: –
* Be a mature Christian with a strong testimony.
* Understand, and fully support, the ethos and vision of the school.
* Be an experienced educator and ideally having previously taught in an IEB school.
* Have excellent organizational and administrative skills.
* Have well-developed people skills & experience in mentoring others.
* Have a track record of positive collaboration within a team.
* Be enthusiastic and able to promote innovation within the subject taught.

To apply, please follow steps below:
Forward your CV, including at least 3 contactable referees, to cv@ShaperHigh.co.za and insert ‘Life Science & Natural Science’ in your e-mail subject line.

Only short-listed applicants will be contacted for an interview. Shaper High reserves the right to not fill this position.

Shaper High, in line with POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act) will attempt to ensure the confidentiality of all applicants for this role. All reasonable measures will be in place to protect personal information, however, it will be used in the recruitment, selection, and reporting process. By submitting your application for this position, you are recognising and accepting this disclaimer.
